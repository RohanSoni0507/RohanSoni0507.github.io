Our Mission

At the Movies and Dramatics Club, our mission is to foster a love for the performing arts within the SRM University community. We aim to:

  • Discover and nurture the innate artistic talents among our students.
  • Provide opportunities for talented individuals to showcase their skills and creativity.
  • Foster the development of essential skills such as communication, teamwork, dialogue, and socialization through the performing arts.
  • Promote cultural and artistic awareness through diverse performances and events.
  • Create a supportive and inclusive environment where all members can thrive and grow as artists.

We believe in the transformative power of the arts and strive to make a positive impact on our members and the wider community through our activities and performances.
