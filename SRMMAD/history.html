Our History

The Movies and Dramatics Club of SRM University was founded in 2014 by a group of passionate students who wanted to create a platform for artistic expression and performance. Over the years, the club has grown significantly, becoming a central part of the university's cultural life.

Milestones

  • 2014: Club founded and first production staged.
  • 2015: First annual theatre festival hosted, attracting participants from various universities.
  • 2017: Collaboration with the Film Club to produce a short film, which won several awards.
  • 2019: Launch of the Summer Acting Workshop series, providing intensive training for aspiring actors.
  • 2020: Adaptation to virtual events and performances during the pandemic, maintaining member engagement and community spirit.
  • 2022: Record-breaking audience attendance for our annual play production.
  • 2024: Celebrating our 10th anniversary with a grand gala and a year-long series of special events.

Throughout our history, we have remained committed to promoting the performing arts and providing a supportive environment for our members to develop their talents. We look forward to continuing this tradition and creating even more memorable experiences in the years to come.
