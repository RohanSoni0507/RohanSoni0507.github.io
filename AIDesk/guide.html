Voice-Activated Personal Assistant - User Guide

Welcome to our project! This guide will help you get started and make the most out of our application.

  • Voice Commands
  • Weather Updates

Getting Started

To begin using our project, simply click the "Start Listening" button, and the application will be ready to receive voice commands. You can also click the "Stop Listening" button to pause voice recognition.

Voice Commands

Our application supports a variety of voice commands, including:

  • "Open YouTube" to open the YouTube website.
  • "Open Google" to open the Google search engine.
  • "Open Stack Overflow" to open the Stack Overflow website.

Weather Updates

You can check the weather in your area by saying "Check Weather." The application will provide you with the current weather conditions, including temperature and description.

News Updates

Stay updated with the latest news by saying "Check News." The application will fetch and display the top news headlines for you.

Math Expressions

If you need to perform calculations, you can say "Calculate" followed by a mathematical expression. Our application will evaluate the expression and provide the result.

Dark Mode

You can customize the visual theme by toggling the "Dark Mode" switch. Enable it for a darker interface or disable it for the default theme.

We hope you find this guide helpful and enjoy using our project. If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to reach out. Thank you for using our application!
