Celebrating 10 Years of Excellence!

We are thrilled to announce that the Movies and Dramatics Club of SRM University is now 10 years old! Over the past decade, we have grown from a small group of passionate individuals to a vibrant community that celebrates the performing arts in all its forms.

Get Involved

We are always looking for new members who share our passion for the performing arts. Whether you want to act, direct, write, or assist behind the scenes, there is a place for you in the Movies and Dramatics Club. Come join us and be part of something special!
