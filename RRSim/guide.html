Fair Play Tournament Manager - User Guide

Getting Started

Welcome to Fair Play Tournament Manager! This guide will help you get started with organizing sports tournaments using our web application.

Step 1: Create a Tournament

Begin by creating a new tournament. You can specify the number of participants and customize the time quantum for scheduling rounds. Once you've set up the basic details, click the "Run Manager" button.

Step 2: Enter Participant Data

For each participant, enter their arrival time and burst time. This data is essential for our round-robin scheduling algorithm to work. You can add as many participants as needed.

Step 3: Run the Scheduler

After entering all participant data, click the "Run Manager" button. Our system will process the information and calculate the scheduling for fair and balanced tournament rounds.

FAQs

Q: How does round-robin scheduling work?

A: Round-robin scheduling ensures that each participant competes against all others in a fair and balanced manner. It divides the tournament into rounds, allowing each participant to face every other participant.

Q: Can I customize the time quantum for rounds?

A: Yes, you can specify the time quantum when creating a tournament. The time quantum determines the maximum time each participant can play in a round before switching to the next.

Q: How are waiting and turnaround times calculated?

A: Waiting time is the time a participant spends waiting for their turn in a round. Turnaround time is the total time a participant takes from arrival to completion of all their matches.
